Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to request the deletion of your personal data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Email Us: Send an email to adielngugi@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."
  2. Include Necessary Information: In your email, please include your username or account email and a brief statement requesting data deletion.
  3. Verification: We may contact you for verification purposes before processing your request.
  4. Processing Time: Once your request is verified, we will process it within 30 days and confirm the deletion via email.
